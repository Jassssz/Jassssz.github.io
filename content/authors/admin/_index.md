## About Me

Yunyi (Jason) Zhu is currently a third-year PhD candidate at the UNSW School of Civil and Environment. His recent research focuses on the development of ML-driven, AR/VR-enabled Digital Twins for performance prediction and optimization of environmental technologies, with a specific emphasis on time-series modelling and causal inference of performance deterioration during long-term field operations. His lifelong motivation is to utilize the skills and technologies to transform how humans perceive and interact with the environment and the world.
